## 📊 Hall of Fame Stats Explained

### Individual Records (Calculated)
- **Highest/lowest voting accuracy (champions):** `max/min(champions, key=voting_accuracy)`
- **Most challenge wins (all):** `max(all_castaways, key=total_wins)`
- **Most/least challenge wins (champions):** `max/min(champions, key=total_wins)`
- **Most/least votes received (champions):** `max/min(champions, key=votes_against)`

### Idol Records (Calculated)
- **Most votes nullified (single idol):** `max(idol_records, key=votes_negated)`
- **Most successful idol plays (player):** Counter of successful plays by player
- **Most idols played (season):** Counter of `day_played` not null per season
- **Most items found (season):** Counter of all advantages per season

### Season Records (Per Season)
- **Items played:** Count of advantages where `day_played` is not null
- **Votes nullified:** Sum of `votes_negated` across all advantages
- **Voted out holding:** Count where `voted_out_holding == true`
- **Players receiving votes:** Unique count of `vote.target` across all tribal councils

**Performance note:** With 3 seasons, all calculations complete in <50ms. Monitor if/when scaled to 39 seasons.
